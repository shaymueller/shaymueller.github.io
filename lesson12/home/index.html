Our Mission

The Provo Chamber of Commerce's mission is to establish local business relationships that promote business growth and support in our community. Members voluntarily give attention to advancing our community for the next generation and inspire many to pursue their dreams in entrepreneurship. This community of volunteers will create a better local economy and sustainability.
