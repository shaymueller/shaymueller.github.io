Site Plan: Audience

Target Audience

My target audience are people in the Southeast Idaho area who travel a lot on the road. They will consist of both female and male users who will mostly use their cell phone to check the weather. These audience member will be 15 and older and will all be of driving age. They will also have goals of coming to the site to check news about traffic in the area.

Personas

Hank

Job: Truck Driver
Demographics: 52 years old, married, father of 2 children, some post-secondary education
Environment: Since he is on the go, he will usually check the website from his mobile phone.

Debbie

Job: Teacher
Demographics: 45 years old, married, mother to teenagers, Bachelor's degree
Environment: She usually has a computer around and checks the website from her laptop.

Senarios

  1. What roads are at risk for black ice on my road trip?
  2. I want to make sure my family member will arrive safely at their destination.
  3. Which days are best for traveling this weekend?
  4. How will the weather affect my commute this morning?
  5. Which route will be best in order to avoid the storm?
